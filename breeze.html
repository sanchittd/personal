Breeze

BreezyBrand is a fashion application concept that allows those that are passionate about fashion trends to be on top of the game. The app allows users to stay up to date with the latest fashion news of their favorite brands/ idols and giving them the freedom of selecting what they want with a guarantee that what they order is always from a verified source and not a replica.
