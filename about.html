Who am I ?

Hi, my name is Sanchitt Dhir.

I'm a Toronto based Junior UI/UX Designer and Front-end Developer. I pride myself on being an easy to work with designer that likes to turn ideas into reality.

Growing up I've always been interested in photography & computers and with a background in computer science I've been fortunate to be able to put those together and truly enjoy what I do. "It's not what you see, it's how you see it".

As Junior UI/UX Desinger & Front-end Developer I'm only just getting started. I genuinely enjoy the process of creating something from nothing. I'm always willing to learn something new and always eager to improve on what I know; you can never know enough.

Apart from my profession, I enjoy traveling, photography, music, working out and being an avid netflix-er.
