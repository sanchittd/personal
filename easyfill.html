EasyFill

EasyFill is a mobile application that helps ease up the process of filling up gas. Users can choose from getting the type of gas they want, how much of it they want and then having the comfort of knowing that the next time they go in their car, the tank will be filled up. Additional offerings include having their car serviced at home and having a luxury car wash.
